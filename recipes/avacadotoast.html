Avacado Toast

Description

Creamy avacado mash on top of perfectly crrusted bread.

Ingredients

  • 2 Avacados
  • Sliced French Bread
  • 2 Tbsp. Butter
  • Red Pepper Flakes
  • 1 lemon
  • salt and pepper (to taste)

Recipe

  1. Peel and mash avacodas. Add juice of lemon. Salt and pepper to taste.
  2. Add butter to pan on medium hight heat. Toast 4 minutes or until golden brown. Flip and toast an additional 2 minutes.
  3. Add mash to top of toast and spread corner to corner.
  4. Top with red pepper flakes, lemon juice and enjoy!
