Brown Sugar Oatmeal

Description

Just oatmeal dummy!

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups Oatmeal
  • 1 cup milk of choice
  • 2 Tbsp. Brown sugar

Recipe

  1. Bring milk to a boil over medium high heat.
  2. Stir in oatmeal and brown sugar and stir until disolved
  3. bring pot down to a simmer for 2 minutes
  4. let cool for 5 minutes and ENJOY!
