Chicken Salad

Description

Copy Cat HEB Chicken Salad Recipe (CLOSE AS YOU CAN GET!)

Ingredients

  • 1 Rotisserie Chicken
  • 2 stalks of Celery
  • 1 cup Mayonnaise
  • 2 tsp Tobasco
  • 1 diced yellow Onion
  • Zapp's Powder Mix (1 pckt.)

Recipe

  1. Pull Chicken meat and add to medium bowl.
  2. Chop and dice celery and onion and add to bowl.
  3. Add mayonnaise, tobasco, and powder mix until well coated
  4. refrigerate for 1 hour and ENJOY!
